About Me

I am a coastal engineer focusing on numerical modeling and applied machine learning to study the impact of sea-level rise driven flooding on coastal communities.

I earned Bachelor's degrees in Civil Engineering and Environmental Engineering at the University of Delaware. I am currently a PhD student on the Coastal Hazards Lab at North Carolina State University.
